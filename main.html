Fish


Most Oregon waters are home to multiple species of fish. Fish play a major role for regional indigenous groups, and are a proud food source. Urbanization and river engineering have dramatically altered fish habitats and migration paths, changing the dynamics of Oregon fish populations. The fish included in this chapter aren't comprehensive. Rather, they are the fish whose habitats have been surveyed by the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife.
